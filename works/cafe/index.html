Our Story

Born from a love
of honest coffee

What started as a tiny roastery in a converted garage has grown into something far greater than we ever imagined.

Cafe Breeze was founded by two friends who shared one obsession: finding the perfect cup of coffee. After years of traveling to origin countries, learning from master roasters, and experimenting endlessly, we opened our doors in 2018 with a simple promise — to serve coffee that honours the craft, the farmers, and the moment.

We source our beans directly from small-hold farms, paying well above fair-trade minimums. Every batch is roasted on-site in our vintage Probat roaster, and every cup is prepared by trained baristas who care deeply about what ends up in your hands.
